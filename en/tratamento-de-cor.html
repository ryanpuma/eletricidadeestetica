Color Grading

Color treatment is an essential process in video post-production that involves correcting and enhancing the colors of a shot to achieve a specific visual result. The importance of color treatment lies in its ability to create a unique atmosphere, enhance visual storytelling and ensure color consistency throughout the video.

Here, the image was treated so that its blown-out digital look was replaced by a more sober, professional-looking balance.

The original footage can be shot in log or non-log format. The log format captures a greater dynamic range, allowing more flexibility in post-production, while non-log formats already apply a specific color profile in the camera, which can result in an image that looks amateurish — yet can still be improved in post-production.
